# 📖 Lexicon Master — SenseEngine

This document serves as a centralized reference for all engine translations. It is used to validate consistency across languages and as a foundation for future expansions.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Translation Philosophy: Sensation over Literality**
> SenseEngine prioritizes the translation of **sensations and onomatopoeias** over the literal meaning of words. We understand the intrinsic complexity of each writing system: from Chinese logograms (Hanzi) and Japanese syllabaries (Katakana for sounds) to Arabic abjads, Indic abugidas (Devanagari/Bengali) and the Cyrillic alphabet.
>
> In many cases, what is a descriptive word in one language is an abstract sound in another (e.g., in Japanese `ボウ` is preferred for the sound of fire instead of `火`). This Lexicon seeks a balance between technical precision and the organic authenticity of sound in each culture.
